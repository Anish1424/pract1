About Us

Amrutvahini College of Engineering (AVCOE), Sangamner was established in 1983 under the leadership of Hon. Late Bhausaheb Thorat and is guided by Hon. Shri. Balasaheb Thorat and Hon. Dr. Sudhir Tambe. It is spread over 15.6 acres, accredited by NBA and NAAC, and certified ISO 9001:2015.

It has a state-of-the-art IT center with 500 Mbps leased line and 300 Mbps broadband. The library holds 66,735 books, 129 journals, and 275 e-journals. AVCOE offers 100% placement assistance, foreign languages (Japanese, German), and quality training to build the future of engineers and entrepreneurs.
